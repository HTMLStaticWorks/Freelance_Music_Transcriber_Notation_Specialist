Pricing FAQ

What defines a "Page" in your pricing?
A page is defined as a standard A4 or Ledger sized sheet music layout with industry-standard staff sizing. We optimize layouts for maximum readability, not page-count inflation.

Do you offer bulk discounts for albums?
Yes. Projects exceeding 50 pages or multi-track album transcriptions are eligible for a 15-20% custom discount. Contact us for a bespoke quote.

Who owns the copyright of the transcriptions?
You do. Upon final payment, you retain 100% ownership and copyright of the digital notation files and any derivative PDF/MIDI data we provide.
